About Us

This website is designed to provide accurate weather forecasting and emergency alerts for conditions like typhoons. Users can check weather forecasts by inputting their location without the need to sign up. Upon registration, users can also receive personalized reminders about weather conditions, along with recommendations for activities such as exercising, hiking, traveling, camping, and surfing.

Additionally, the platform offers features like saving searches, recording notes for selected hotels and transportation, and accessing emergency numbers during natural disasters. An admin dashboard is also available to manage user information and their interactions with the platform.
